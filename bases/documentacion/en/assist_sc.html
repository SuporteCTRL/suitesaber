Subfield filing assistant

Use  to see the help menu for the field

The field occurrences are shown in the display menu located next to the name of the field.

Use:

 to add a new occurrence

 to delete the selected occurrence from the display menu

 to move the selected occurrence up in the display menu

 to move the selected occurrence down in the display menu

 

To create a new occurrence click on  and use the form that appears in the window. To edit an already existing occurrence click on it in the corresponding menu.  During the editing use:

The subfields menu to insert new subfields for the occurrence at hand

 to move a subfield up

 to move a subfield down

 To consult the associated list, authority list, thesaurus or dictionary of terms associated to the corresponding field

 

  When you finish modifying the occurrence, use:

 to cancel the changes

 to accept the new occurrence or the changes made on one already existing. In the display menu you will see the new occurrence or the changes you made

 to pass the field with all its occurrences to the entry form

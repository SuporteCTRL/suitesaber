Notas :
  • This option retrieves title words, words from the abstract, and keywords.
  • Use truncation symbol $(dollar sign) to search words with the same root. Example: educ$ retrieves educación, education, educação, etc.
  • Do not type boolean operators (AND, OR ou AND NOT) between words.
  • Select the option All words (AND) to link the words (reduce the scope of the retrieval), or the option Any word (OR) to add words (enlarge the scope of the retrieval).
  • To search over other fields or to specify the field of the search use the Advanced form.
